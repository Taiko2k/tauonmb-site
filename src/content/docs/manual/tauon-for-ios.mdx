Tauon for iOS is a companion mobile music player app with a similar *folders as albums* design to Tauon on Desktop.

The app is currently in development, with a public beta available on [TestFlight](https://testflight.apple.com/join/5w47Upm1).
Note that app is heavily in beta, and may be changed or removed in the future.

### Main Modes

1. Import folders locally on your phone.
2. Connect to Tauon Desktop to remote-control playback.
3. Stream audio from Tauon Desktop to your phone.

### How To Remote Control

1. Enable `Enable remote control` in Tauon: `Menu > Settings > Function Page 4`, then restart Tauon.
2. In Tauon for iOS, enable `Remote Connection` and enter your host PC IP address.
3. Select a remote playlist from the list, then swipe right to view albums.
4. Disable `Remote Connection` when done. Any saved playlists will remain.
