## Cast to a device

<Steps>
1. Go to `MENU` > `Chromecast...` and select your device.
2. Confirm **Chromecast mode** appears in the top panel, then use playback controls as normal.
3. To stop casting, return to `MENU` > `Chromecast...` and select **End cast**.
</Steps>

:::note
If you turned on your Chromecast after starting Tauon and it doesn’t appear, restart Tauon.
:::

:::tip
If you have a firewall on the same PC, allow incoming as well as outgoing connections for Tauon.
:::

## Limitations

- Doesn’t work with PLEX, Airsonic, Jellyfin, or Spotify.
- Doesn’t work with some codecs (transcoding isn’t implemented). Common codecs like MP3, FLAC, and OGG should work.
