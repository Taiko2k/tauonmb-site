Effective date: 01, March, 2026

Tauon for iOS is designed to play music from selected folders you choose on your device and optionally connect to your own Tauon Desktop instance on your local network.

1. **Data Collection:**
Tauon for iOS does not collect, track, sell, or share personal data.
We do not run analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking.

2. **Data Stored on Device:**
The app stores library information (for example, folder paths, album/track metadata, artwork cache, and playback state) locally on your device to provide app functionality.
This data stays on your device unless you remove the app or clear app data.

3. **Network Use:**
If you enable Remote Connection, Tauon for iOS connects only to the IP/host you enter for your Tauon Desktop instance.
No music library or personal data is sent to developer-operated servers.

4. **Permissions:**
Folder access is used only to scan and play music from locations you select.

5. **Children’s Privacy:**
Tauon for iOS does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

6. **Changes to This Policy:**
If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted with a new effective date.
